Effect of the Student grade according to Different Variables¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look on how different conditions affect our students grade and how to apply them. Which can give us a better results for our children, kids, students ..etc. The main focus in this investigation is on the four conditions that affect our students for a better grades in "Math", "Writing", and "Reading" test.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consisted of 1,000 student with 8 columns. The attributes that affect the student Race/Ethnicity, Gender, Parental level, Lunch, and Preperation Test and the outcome of the student grades in "Math", "Reading",and "Writing" test. No data has been removed as their was no null values or duplicates

In the math test our grades are normally distrubuted and can be clarify not many conditions affect their grades. On the other hand, the Writing and Reading test has a more spikey values make us wonder why their is sudden fall in grades and how to fix these problems?.
